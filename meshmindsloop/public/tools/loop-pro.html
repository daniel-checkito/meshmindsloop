⚠️ Read before downloading
  • Always supervise your printer during the first full run
  • PLA + Textured PEI only — PETG will not release and may damage your printer
  • Tilt printer ~20° so parts slide into a collection box
  • If parts stick, increase cooldown time or lower room temperature
  • Tall prints risk collision with the eject sweep — check Z height warnings
  • Repeated ejections cause wear on the print head over time
